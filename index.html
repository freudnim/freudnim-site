Terms of Service

Workflow

「 ⏰ Planning 」

  • Kindly provide any reference images and/or descriptions.
  • Example images of a specific pose/expression/composition that you want are much appreciated!

「 ✏️ Sketches & Revisions 」

  • I’ll send you sketch(es) and we can discuss any adjustments.

「 📄 Invoice 50% 」

  • I’ll send an invoice over PayPal with a 50% minimum payment due on receipt.

「 💨 Art Progress 」

  • I’ll let you know an estimated timeline in which I can get back to you with the finished illustration (can range from a few days to one month).

「 🎨 Artwork + Invoice 100% 」

  • I’ll finish the artwork and send a watermarked version of the final piece. Upon receiving full payment, I’ll send the non-watermarked version over a Google Drive folder.
  • I ask that you pay the remaining balance within 7 days.

✅ Will do

  • Vtubers!
  • Fanart
  • OCs

❌ Will not do

  • NSFW
  • Complex backgrounds
  • Machinery
  • Offensive material

Payment

  • I currently accept PayPal only, via invoices in USD.
  • At least 50% must be paid upfront.
  • If, for some reason, I wouldn’t be able to finish the commission, I will give a full refund, but I won’t give refunds for finished works.
